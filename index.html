Lessons

Are you a casual swimmer looking for a way to improve your swimming skills? A triathlete that could use a technique upgrade? A student that just wants to learn to swim fluently?

In short, you want to take your swimming technique and/or endurance to a higher level. Then you have found the right place to accomplish just that! We offer courses comprised of ten lessons that aim to enhance our members technique in all four swimming styles. Our personalized approach is backed with technical knowledge and hands on experience.

Start to swim does not offer lessons for kids. However we can refer you to Abdelaziz Sahli.
