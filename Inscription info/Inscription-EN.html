How to register?

Easy! Six steps:

  • Consider your swimming level

    This series is meant for adults that already know the basics and not for absolute beginners. Can you swim 200M in breaststroke and crawl without stopping? Then you are good to go!

  • Contact us

    Through the website or info@starttoswim.eu (mail). Indicate your swimming level and main goal.

  • Wait for confirmation

    Someone will get back to you to confirm your inscription

  • Complete payment

    Transfer 115€ one week before the first lesson on the account number: BE57 3101 1693 8135 or pay in cash on the first lesson. Communication: "Start to swim – series 2, 2021 - Name + First name".

  • Check your swimming gear

    A swimming trunk, cap, glasses and towel will suffice!

  • Start to swim!

    Start your sunday with a splash!
